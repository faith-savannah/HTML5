Have you ever had one of those days or even weeks when you thought nothing is going in your favour - days where you wish you rewind time and just start over again? Days where you wish you could just hit the reset button? Don't worry, we've all been there. The good news is that even though there is no magical button you can press, there is yoga and yoga can help "reset" you on those less than steller days.

Calm your mind and be with Steven Espinosa. When we come to our mats it is opportunity for us to move from constantly "doing" into a place of "being". During this class we will focus on being in our breath, our body and in out heart through a well rounded, full spectrum mixed level class. Starting with breath work to calm out mind, then moving to a series of gentle Warm Ups into Standing Poses, Inversions, Hip Openers and Back Bends.

Reset your energy with Kia Miller A meditation accompanied by live music for self regeneration and healing whenever you need to reset your energy and take a moment for yourself.
